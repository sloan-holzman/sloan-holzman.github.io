Virtual Wallet

MERN stack (MongoDB, Express.js, React.js, and Node.js)

Website that allows users to save virtual copies (including auto-generated bar codes) of their gift cards and coupons, so that users do not have to keep track of physical copies. The site uses Twitter access tokens for 3rd party authentication, a web scraper to provide the urls of over 2,000 retailer websites where users can check their gift card balances, and links to sites where users can find specific deals for their retailer of interest.
